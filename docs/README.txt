ZUGZWANG 1.0.0
Professional Lead Generation & Recruitment Research Tool

OVERVIEW:
ZUGZWANG is a surgical, high-density dashboard designed for rapid lead extraction 
and recruitment automation. 

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
- Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
- ~1GB free disk space
- No separate Playwright installation required (bundled)

QUICK START:
1. Launch ZUGZWANG from the desktop shortcut or Start Menu.
2. Enter your Developer Master Key if prompted.
3. Configure your search parameters in the "Jobsuche" tab.
4. Click "Start Extraction" to begin harvesting leads.
